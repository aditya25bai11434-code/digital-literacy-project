# Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist for College Students

1. Never click on links or attachments from unknown emails/WhatsApp.
2. Always check the URL before entering any password (look for “https://” and correct spelling).
3. Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication everywhere.
4. Never share your OTP or banking PIN with anyone (not even friends).
5. Verify UPI requests by calling the person instead of just clicking “Pay”.
6. Install official apps only from Google Play Store or Apple Store.
7. Keep your phone and laptop updated with latest security patches.
8. Use Google’s “Safe Browsing” or any antivirus on your device.

**Special UPI / Financial Safety Tips:**
- Never scan unknown QR codes for payments.
- Set UPI transaction limit to minimum amount.

**If you become a victim:**
Report immediately on National Cyber Crime Portal → https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Call Helpline → 1930 (24×7)

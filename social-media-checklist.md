# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's ✅
1. Think before posting – once it’s online, it stays forever.
2. Use privacy settings properly.
3. Post positive and helpful content.
4. Respect others’ opinions in comments.
5. Keep your profile professional for future jobs.

## Don'ts ❌
1. Don’t share your location or personal phone number publicly.
2. Never post while angry or emotional.
3. Don’t bully or spread rumours about anyone.
4. Avoid sharing fake news without checking.
5. Don’t use abusive language in any post or comment.
